autoresearch campaign viewer

Offline inspector for campaigns/<slug>/<battery>.json files written by micro_runner.py. Load two or more batteries for a control-vs-variant comparison. Click a case row to fold open its reps. When served over HTTP (e.g. python3 -m http.server), batteries can also be preloaded via ?files=path/a.json,path/b.json.
